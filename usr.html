About This Site

Mostly what I hope to accomplish with this site is to share music and software I personally use and think others should consider using. Additionally, I think it is also healthy (and hopefully fun) to articulate and record how one feels periodically.

The site is intended to have a minimalistic approach. The amount of styling is kept to a bare minimum while maintaining the general aesthetic. Additionally, thus far I have not used any Javascript by design.

Two things worth mentioning

First, I'm writing everything in a programming text editor which doesn't have spell checker (at least I haven't enabled it), so expect some poor grammar. I don't really care too much for mistakes contained within. If you do, feel free to submit a pull request, and I'll gladly merge it.

Second, I want the content on here to be more styled more like a diary. Most of the dialog will try to be stream-of-conscience style. I might say something stupid and it very well be something I disagree with the next day, so it's worth mentioning now that I generally tend not to say things out of malice. If there is something you find controversial or offensive, please reach out to me personally and in private first and we can discuss further why I felt that way and why if I still do. If you don't care for anything I have to say, I encourage you not to visit again.
